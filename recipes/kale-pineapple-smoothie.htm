Description: It took a while to get into the green smoothie trend. We much preferred a sweet tasting berry burst, but this delicious glass of green goodness is divine. The pineapple chunks add a delicious sweet taste to the drink so it doesn't taste as bitter as other green smoothies. It makes the perfect healthy breakfast or post-workout snack.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 ½ cups fresh pineapple chunks
  • ½ (16 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup chopped kale

Directions: Place pineapple, coconut milk, banana, and kale in a blender; blend until smooth.
