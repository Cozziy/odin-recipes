Description:This delicious green smoothie has pineapple, mango, banana, blueberries, and greens of your choice.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • ¾ cup water
  • 1 medium banana, broken into chunks
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup frozen pineapple
  • ½ cup frozen mango

Directions: Combine kale and water in a blender; process until no chunks remain. Add banana, blueberries, pineapple, and mango; blend until smooth.
